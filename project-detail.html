Overview :

A compact living space designed to maximize functionality through efficient planning and minimal detailing. The project focuses on clarity, simplicity, and intelligent use of space to support modern urban living.

Constraints :

The limited area required multifunctional solutions and careful zoning to avoid clutter. Storage, circulation, and usability were key challenges addressed through thoughtful design.

Outcome :

The final space achieves a balance between efficiency and aesthetics, offering a clean and highly functional environment. Every element contributes to a sense of order and usability without compromising visual simplicity.
